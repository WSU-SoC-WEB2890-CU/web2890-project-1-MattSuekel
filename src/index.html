How It Works

The process of selling your car with Cardeal is easy and straightforward. Our employees will keep you informed so you understand what happens next.

  1. 1

    Fill out the form (i.e., VIN, year, make, model, mileage, condition, accidents)

  2. 2

    Get an instant offer on your car good for 7 days

  3. 3

    Provide access: deliver to the nearest Cardeal location or schedule pickup

  4. 4

    Bring the title proving your ownership

  5. 5

    We inspect your car to verify its condition

  6. 6

    Accept the offer for cash or apply it to a trade-in
